Dear Users

At FoodLens, we do our best to classify restrictive ingredients accurately based on generic recipes. However, it's important to keep in mind that chefs often have their own unique variations and may adjust recipes to suit their specialty.

Because of this, we recommend that our valued customers always ask the restaurant or chef for specific details regarding any dietary restrictions. This will ensure you have the most up-to-date and accurate information about the ingredients used in your meal.

Your health and satisfaction are our top priorities, and by asking questions, you can make more informed choices that align with your dietary preferences and requirements.

Furthermore, The SnapPlate is still at it's early stage, and currenly it can detect upto 239 foods and we plan to develop the model further. We hope that, we can earn your support during this time

Enjoy your dining experience, and feel free to reach out to us if you have any questions or concerns.
